# RACH support – visit agenda
 
## Introduction

Our key messaging/purpose as RACH relationship managers: 
We are here to build stronger connection with Residential Aged Care Homes across our footprint. We want to understand what’s working well, where the challenges are, and how we can support better care through collaboration. 
 
What we do: 
* Improve access to GPs and allied health 
* Reduce unnecessary hospital transfers 
* Strengthen dementia care pathways 
* dentify health needs early 
* Work with local health services, RACHs, and community partners 
 
 
## Support and Opportunities Available – Overview 
 
### EPCA Grants 
    • Grants up to $90,000 per project to improve primary care access in aged care homes. 
    • Purpose: 
    • Support innovative, local solutions in “thin markets” 
    • Build primary care capacity or strengthen aged care homes’ ability to meet residents’ needs 
    • Project Ideas: 
    • Virtual, hybrid, or multidisciplinary care models 
    • Capacity-building initiatives 
    • Must demonstrate collaboration, co-design, cultural safety, and sustainability 
    • Key Dates: 
    • EOIs close: 9am, 31 October 2025 
    • Industry briefing: 7 October 2025, 10:30am 
    • Apply via: Tenderlink (details on Healthy North Coast website) 
 
 
### Building Nurse Capacity for Healthy Ageing Education Modules 
 
    • Overview 
    • Free education initiative for nurses in RACHs 
    • Strengthens capability, confidence, and proactive care 
    • Flexible, bite-sized learning 
    • Includes support: coaching circles, CoP events, HealthPathways access 
 
    • Modules: 
    • Deteriorating Resident Triage Tool 
    • Advance Care Planning 
    • Palliative Care 
    • My Health Record
